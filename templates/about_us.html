About Us

Our team recently came up with an idea to create this page as many world disasters are occuring more and more frequently these days. This page is a way for people to stay connected no matter where they are in the world!

Description of Links in the Navigation Bar

Register

 You must register before you can login and access the Check-In and Search features

Login In

 After you register, you must login to access the Check-In and Search features

Check-In

 Check in with your demographics and a brief description of how you are doing. Feel free to upload a photo of yourself if you would like as well!

Search

 Search for someone you are concerned for. Make sure that you know their first and last name!

Home

 Clicking Home at the top will take you back to the home page!
